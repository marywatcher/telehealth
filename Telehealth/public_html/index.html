What is Tutio Health?

Tutio Health is an online tele-health platform for patients with a wide range of neurologic conditions in need of assistance from cognitive therapy, speech therapy, language training, and various aspects of physical and occupational therapy. Our platform connects patients with highly accredited practitioners and certified healthcare professionals, occupational therapists, speech language pathologists, and licensed nurses. Tutio Health is also a database for patients to upload their own medical information to better unite them with a provider that will accurately suit their needs. Practitioners have access to these medical profiles, allowing them to customize a management plan that will accurately treat and support patients. Our online features personalize care with live practitioner-to-patient sessions through video conferencing, a whiteboard, and chat functions. Of course, Tutio Health is fully HIPAA compliant.

- Why Tutio Health? -

Unique Online Learning Technology

Sessions occur in our customized video conference with two way whiteboard, chat, and screen sharing capabilities. Tutio Health strives for our patients to have the feeling of a private evaluation or consultation in the comfort of their own home.

  • Tracking System

    Patients can upload medical history, results from test, and any other relevant information that may help their progress within our sessions. This information is stored on our HIPAA compliant platform where the health practitioner can use it to address specific issues. In the post sessions, email updates are sent to the patients and/or their doctors. All these updates are also stored on our each patient's profile.

  • Flexibility

    Tutio Health allows patients to schedule sessions from the comfort of their own home and at their own schedule. Our online sessions reduce logistical issues and wait times you may experience in the hospital setting or when trying to get to a therapy facility.

  • Live sessions

    Sessions are conducted and led by a live highly accredited practitioner.

- How it works -

For patients

  • Sign up and log into Tutio Health
  • Upload medical information such as goals and objectives
  • Search health practitioner's profile which includes: experience, education, and short video.
  • Choose health practitioner and schedule a time to meet.
  • Sessions occur with video conference and whiteboard.
  • Rate your health practitioner.

For health practitioners

  • Sign up
  • Phone conversation and a mock session are conducted.
  • Get put in our database.
  • Once approved, you are added to Tutio database.
  • Set your own schedule of availability.
  • Live session occurs with video conference and shared whiteboard.
  • Complete post session notes which is sent to guardian/doctor or contact person.
  • Get paid bi-monthly.
